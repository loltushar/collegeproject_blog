THE VALORANT CHAMPIONS TOUR

The Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) is a global competitive esports tournament series for the video game Valorant organised by Riot Games, the game's developers. The series runs multiple events throughout each season, culminating in Valorant Champions, the top-level event of the tour. GLOBAL ESPORTS REPRESENTING INDIA

Global Esports is a Premium Esports Organization based out of India. GE has a heritage of almost half a decade with its presence in over 19 countries in PC, Mobile & Console games spread over 26 rosters. Global Esports became one of the 30 teams in the world who reserved their slot for the VCT 2023: Pacific League. In a recent post by @ValorantEsports on Twitter, Riot Games confirmed 30 teams worldwide who will compete in Valorant Champions Tour 2023.
